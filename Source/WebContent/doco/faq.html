FAQ

Description
This tweak creates a clickable FAQ section. Answers are hidden until it's associated question is clicked.
Skill Level
Easy
How to Use
  1. Create a new item, and ensure the letters 'FAQ' are somewhere in the item's Title.
  2. In the Text Editor, write your questions followed immediatley by their answers on the next line. Ensure that your questions are in bold format.

    3. eg:

    I am a question.

    And I am an Answer.

    Are you the king of questions?

    No, I'm the king of answers.

  3. Save your FAQ and test to see if the answers appear when clicked.
