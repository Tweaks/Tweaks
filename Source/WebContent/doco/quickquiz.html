Quick Quiz

Description
This tweak lets you embed a quick formative quiz in line with your content.
Skill Level
Medium (Simple HTML)
How to Use
  1. Create a new item in Blackboard, or edit an existing one.
  2. On a new line, type in your question text such as "What is the capital city of Australia?"
  3. Immediately below each question, insert a table with 2 columns and a row for each possible answer.

    eg:

    This is a quiz question.  What is the answer?
    Is the answer A? No. Try once more.
    Is the answer B? No - have another try!
    Is the answer C? Correct - C is the answer.
  4. In the first column, write in a possible answer. In the adjacent column, write the feedback you wish to provide for this answer
  5. Turn on HTML mode, and add the following DIV tag around your question text to activate it: 
    <div class="quiz">What is the capital city of Australia?</div>

  6. Save your quick quiz question.

Note: This quiz does not make any attempt to record answers. Use it to provide a self-check of understanding content and concepts
